# 🏳️🔍 Flags and Culture at Your Fingertips 📱
**Explore the world like never before**. This web application offers an immersive experience for discovering countries, their flags, and key facts visually, tactilely, and in a fully bilingual (Spanish/English) format. Designed with a mobile-first approach, it works perfectly on mobile phones, tablets, and computers.

# 🌍 Interactive World Atlas
## ✨ Main Features

### 🎡 Natural Gesture Country Carousel
- **Swipe horizontally** (touch or mouse) to change countries, or use the **side arrows**.

- Each card displays the flag in large size, the official name, capital, languages, and continent.

- The background colors change dynamically depending on the country, creating a unique and engaging visual experience.

### ⭐ Persistent Favorites System
- Mark your favorite countries with just a tap of the star (★/☆).

- All favorites are automatically saved to the **browser's local storage** – they are not lost when you close the app.

- Quickly access them in the bottom section, and from there you can:

- Navigate directly to the country.

- Delete each favorite individually (✖ button).

- Clear the entire list with a single click.

### 🌐 Bilingual with Slide Switch
- Switch between **Spanish** and **English** with a sleek slide switch (you can also click or drag).

- Country names, continents, languages, and tags are translated instantly.

- Even native languages ​​(like "German" → "Alemán") are automatically adapted.

### 🖼️ Full-Screen Flag Zoom
- Tap any flag to view it in a **zoomed modal** with a blurred background.

- Ideal for appreciating the details of each design.

- Close by tapping outside the image, pressing the 'X', or the **ESC** key.

### 📊 Accurate Data from Official Sources
- The app uses the **Countries REST API** (v3.1), obtaining up-to-date information: capital, languages ​​spoken, continent, and flag in SVG/PNG format.

- Countries are listed alphabetically by their Spanish names for more intuitive navigation.

## 🎨 Design and User Experience
- **Clean and modern interface** with gradients, transparencies, and background filter effects.

- **Fully responsive**: adapts to any screen size, from narrow mobile phones to wide monitors.

- **Tap feedback**: buttons shrink when pressed, and arrows respond instantly.

- **Dynamic contrast**: text color automatically adjusts to the background to ensure readability.

- **No external dependencies**: all code (HTML, CSS, JS) is in a single file, ready to use without installation or frameworks.

## 🧭 How to use the app
1. **Navigate**: swipe or use the arrows ◀ ▶ to explore the countries.

2. **Favorites**: Tap the star in the upper right corner of the card. Your favorites will appear below.

3. **Enlarge Flag**: Tap any flag image to view it full screen.

4. **Change Language**: Slide or tap the "Spanish/English" switch.

5. **Go to a Favorite**: Click on its card in the bottom section.

6. **Delete Favorites**: Tap the '✖' on each card, or use "Clear" to delete them all.

## 🚀 Technologies Used
- Semantic HTML5 + CSS3 (flex, grid, animations, media queries)
- Modern JavaScript (ES6+): async/await, localStorage, touch and mouse events
- REST API: `https://restcountries.com/v3.1/all` (optimized fields)
